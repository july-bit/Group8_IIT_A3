Group Reflection

Whole Group

For this course, our group members have a lot to learn from one another because this is our first time working together. Even though everything went according to plan, there are still a few things that could have been done more efficiently and effectively. As we have stressed throughout the course, effective communication and collaboration are critical components of successfully completing a task on time. Recognizing the importance of this factor, we will talk about our team's progress throughout the course, as well as our long-term objectives. To begin, our communication is flawless for a team that has only recently established itself in this course, we never miss meetings, and our team consistently completes work ahead of schedule. Additionally, we usually have a meeting together outside of school to strengthen our ties and improve our communication with and among one another. As a result of everyone contributing their best efforts and assisting one another in overcoming our individual weaknesses, we are pleased with the outcome of our project. Despite our team's high level of achievement, there is always room for improvement. When it comes to decision-making, we frequently pause and take an extended period of time to reach a conclusion due to our inexperience and lack of familiarity with one another. We were surprised at how close we had become after completing the course. We have developed into more than teammates; we have developed into friends, which our group considers a success. We know that we can rely on one another for the next couple of courses, and our teamwork will undoubtedly improve as a result. After a period of collaboration, we determined that adhering to deadlines and holding weekly meetings are critical components of any project. Deadlines help us plan for the future and brainstorm what we need to accomplish throughout the week. Additionally, we learn that setting aside some spare time is necessary due to the frequency with which problems arise during an assignment. Additionally, having a weekly meeting on a specific day is critical because it enables us to monitor the project's progress while also discussing what we should and should not do to improve the overall quality of the project. Additionally, it aids us in resolving any issues that arise as a team, ensuring that the solutions are optimal. After completing this course, we will have gained practical experience working in teams, particularly in the information technology industry, and we will be able to devise a more effective method of collaboration for future projects.

Le Duc Quang Vu

But when all is said and done, this was arguably, one of the best team-working experiences I have ever had at RMIT, and I wish my underclassmen more successful projects and/or assignments to come.

Luong Thao My

In general, I believe that the group as a whole was extremely dedicated and committed to bringing the somewhat implausible game concept to life. If I had to choose a single word to describe our entire group work experience, it would be "Harmonious." Since the group's inception, during the idea generation and research phase, our team has been extremely respectful of one another's opinions and willing to expand our idea options. However, our mutual regard for one another inevitably complicates decision-making. This occurs as a result of everyone's compliant and general positivity with the proposed topics, not leaning toward any specific idea. Thus, having an individual with strong preferences for a particular option was critical in order to provide counter arguments for others and motivate the team to think more critically. With regards to the project's concept, because our chosen subject denotes a fairly complex and involved subject, our team has encountered several difficulties collaborating and resolving inevitable problems. Choosing the most appropriate game engines proved to be one of the most difficult aspects. Because the same situation of indecision could have occurred during this phase, I proposed that the game development team (Thu, Tri, and me) each conduct independent research on the various game engine options and be prepared to discuss several critical points, such as whether we want to create an app or a web game, the advantages and disadvantages of each popular and well-known game engine, and whether our game requires specific specialized features.
